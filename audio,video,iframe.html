This article will tell you everything about the (now discontinued) Legacy console edition of minecraft.

This edition was made as a way to play minecraft on the following consoles:

  • Xbox 360
  • PS3
  • Wii U
  • New nintendo 3ds
  • Nintendo Switch
  • PS4
  • Xbox One
  • PS Vita
Using backwards compatibility it can be played on Nintendo switch 2,Xbox X/S and PS5 These editions(Frequently bundled together as Legacy console edition) were the most celebrated versions among the playerbase. They were made in a partnership between Mojang(The company that owns minecraft) and another company called 4J Studios.
